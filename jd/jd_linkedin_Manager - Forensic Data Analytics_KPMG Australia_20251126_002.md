### ABOUT THE JOB / JOB DESCRIPTION

Company Description

Our Forensic team assists clients in detecting and responding to fraud, bribery, corruption, money laundering, misconduct, and other financial crime issues, as well as resolving commercial disputes. We help clients protect their businesses by identifying discrepancies in facts and figures, non-compliant behaviours, and advising on legal and regulatory requirements. Applying forensic techniques, investigation, intelligence, technology, and industry expertise, we help prevent and resolve commercial disputes, fraud, misconduct, and regulatory breaches.

Job Description

As a Forensic Data Analytics Manager specialising in wage compliance, you will play a pivotal role in helping our clients identify and remediate breaches against an employer’s Enterprise Agreement, Modern Award, National Employment Standards or jurisdictional based long service leave legislation. This role is open to both Managers and Senior Consultants ready to take the next step in their career.

We’re looking for passionate data analysts driven by purpose and precision to make a real difference in people’s lives. Payroll data analytics isn’t just about numbers, it’s about making sure every worker receives their fair entitlements.

You will leverage your experience in business analysis, data investigation, and analytic skills to identify, investigate, quantify, and report on wage underpayment matters.

Working with a diverse team of data analysts and subject matter specialists, your work will help uncover discrepancies, support ethical business practices, and uphold the rights of workers across Australia.

Your Opportunity

Lead the technical design and development of analysis models (SQL or Excel) to identify and quantify wage compliance risks.

Work with subject matter specialists to understand the business requirements for modelling, including resolution of ambiguous requirements and identification of relevant data sources.

Collaborate with clients to identify root causes of wage compliance issues and establish plans for remediation.

Conduct testing and provide guidance and coaching to junior team members.

Document results and prepare working papers and reports to support analysis.

Support proposal development and other business development activities.

Assist in developing internal data analytics assets and tools.

Your Experience

Experience in analysis of large and complex payroll and time & attendance data to identify or quantify wage compliance issues.

At least 4 years of experience in development of SQL, Alteryx or Excel data models.

Proven track record of leading small teams of data analysts, including task assignment and quality review.

Skilled in translating complex business and legal requirements into technical specifications for analysis and preparation of written reports / methodologies for presentation to regulators and legal representatives.

Strong project management skills.

Background in professional services is preferred.

Qualifications

Desired tertiary qualifications in a combination of business disciplines (e.g., finance, accounting, law) and technical disciplines (e.g., engineering, IT, computing).
